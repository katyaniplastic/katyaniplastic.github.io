Common questions

FAQs

What happens during the first visit?

The first consultation is a detailed conversation — your concerns, history, sleep, routine and stresses. It is completely confidential. You'll leave with a clear understanding of what's going on and a treatment plan.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes. Video consultations are available, and follow-ups can often be done online. WhatsApp us to schedule one.

Is my visit confidential?

Absolutely. Everything discussed stays between you and the doctor, as required by medical ethics and law. Seeking help for mental health is a sign of strength, not weakness.

What is the consultation fee?

The fee is shared when you book — call or WhatsApp +91 83684 96558 and we'll confirm the fee and the earliest available slot.

Can I come with a family member?

Yes, you are welcome to bring a family member — especially for child, adolescent and elderly consultations, where the family's involvement often helps treatment.

In a crisis? If you or someone with you is in immediate danger or having thoughts of self-harm, please call the national Tele-MANAS mental-health helpline 14416 (24×7, free) or emergency services 112 right away.
